Dystopian Books To Read This Summer

Matty - Sat Jul 8, 2023

Cinder - Marissa Meyer

In this futuristic retelling of Cinderella,
follow the captivating journey of Cinder,
a cyborg mechanic with a mysterious past,
as she uncovers secrets that could alter
the destiny of not just her own world,
but that of the entire galaxy.

Skyhunter - Marie Lu

Set in a dystopian future where humanity
is under threat from monstrous creatures,
join Talin, a fierce young warrior,
as she fights to protect her people
against the relentless enemy.

Warcross - Marie Lu

Emika Chen, a talented hacker and bounty hunter,
as she is thrust into the glitz and glamour of the
competitive Warcross Championships. As Emika delves
deeper into this high-stakes world, she uncovers a
dark conspiracy that could have unimaginable consequences.

Red Queen - Victoria Aveyard

In a society where those with red blood serve the ruling
class of silver-blooded elites, Mare Barrow's life changes
forever. When she discovers that she possesses a deadly
ability despite her red blood, Mare becomes a pawn in a
dangerous game of politics and rebellion that threatens
to shake the foundations of their entire world.

Renegades - Marissa Meyer

In a society where prodigies possess extraordinary abilities,
a fierce battle rages between the Renegades, and the Anarchists,
the rebels fighting for freedom. Nova, a young prodigy
with a tragic past, and Adrian, the son of two legendary Renegades.
As their paths collide, secrets are unveiled and alliances are tested.
