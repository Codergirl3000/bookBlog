American Panda by Gloria Chao

Calista - Sat Jul 8, 2023

Introduction

American Panda is a book for teenagers to relate to, growing up with strict parents and high expectations. The main character, Mei, finds herself hiding behind a mask whenever she is around her parents.

Why is this a good book

Mei is a character that I think many people can at least, somewhat relate to. It is an amazing book that will keep you hooked until the end. This book takes you on an adventure of how it is like to live Mei’s life – and the many struggles she faces, including her feelings for Darren, rebelling against her parents, and trying to get rid of her incredibly bad phobia of germs.

Overall

Age range: 12+
Plot: 9/10
Characters: 8/10
Antagonists: 8/10
Protagonists: 8.5/10
Character developments: 10/10
Re-readability: 7/10
Rating: 8.416/10
