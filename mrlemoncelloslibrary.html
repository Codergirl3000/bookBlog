Mr. Lemoncello's Library by Chris Grabenstein

Gwen - Mon Aug 7, 2023

Okay, I LOVED this book when I was younger, and so I was extremely excited to learn about the movie adaptation. However, bluntly, it was absolutely terrible.
A favorite part of the book was the puzzles- there’s even a puzzle to solve after reading the book. (I solved this and emailed the author and he responded!) I found the puzzles in the movie HORRIBLE. You didn’t get the same sense of following along and the gratification of solving the puzzles.

Interesting Facts

  • Book is rated 4.13 on goodreads (this is out of 5 stars)
  • Movie is rated 43% on rotten tomatoes
  • Movie was not box office as it is a nickelodeon movie
  • 5 books in main series with an additional prequel

  • Overall rating- Book 8/10. It was a pretty good book and I read all of the series. Would recommend it for younger readers.
  • Overall rating- Movie 1/10. Visually it wasn’t terrible, but the quality of acting, quality of movie in general and plot/storyline was just atrocious. Would NOT recommend it. FAR better movies exist.
